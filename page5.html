What jobs can one get after BS in Hospitality and Tourism Management?

BS in Hospitality and Tourism Management is a fairly new line of formal study. This newness often brings with it hefty amounts of doubt about its usefulness in the present climate.

To put these concerns to rest we have created this comprehensive piece on what this degree entails and what is the hospitality market like in Pakistan.

The subjects that are taught in this degree program include:

  • Cultural Tourism
  • Commercial Aviation
  • Presentation Skills
  • Organizational and Consumer Behaviour
  • Airport Planning and Management
  • Housekeeping, etc.

    • Your job options include, but are not limited to:

  • Event Planner
  • Hotel Manager
  • Tour Manager
  • Customer Service Personnel
  • Air Crew

    • Jobs are available in both the public and private sectors.

    Who should go for this degree?

    This line of work and education is best suited to those who have great organizational skills and derive fulfillment from arrangement and order.

    Strong communication skills are also a requirement for this field. Talking to others, understanding their requirements and offering them solutions and ideas all mean that you should be able to effectively communicate.

    When your work revolves around other people and engaging with them effectively, a patient and empathetic temperament are key.

    Furthermore, if you want to work in the tourism industry then you should be well traveled or at least have the desire to be. This is because traveling can be extremely hectic if you are not enjoying it and then trying to make a career out of it can prove to be extremely challenging.

    If these above mentioned characteristics are your strengths then you will most likely enjoy working in this field.

    Remember that since this is a life-long choice you are making, ensure that you are doing it for the right reasons.

    Don’t rush into a potential career as a spur of the moment decision. If you put enough thought into it now it will save you a lot of time and energy in the future.

    Whether or not this is a lucrative career is a very relative question. That’s because the opportunities a person is given and where they go from there will vary from one to the other. But is there room to turn this into a high-paying source of income? Yes, there is definitely room. Whether or not you make it happen depends on your personal initiative.

    Business Opportunities

    When you have the knowledge and required skills for event management and planning you can even look into starting a small business. The good thing about that is that there is the very little cost required for set up.

    You will be offering a service and the expenses for the event will be paid by the client. This leaves you with the work and organization only. If you know how to do that, this arrangement could really work for you.

    There is no shortage of ceremonies and events after all.

    In a nutshell, this line of work can be very interesting and lucrative if you are a people person and good at planning and organization.

    Good luck!
